2022 4DN Annual Meeting

Marriott Marquis San Diego Marina
December 7-9, 2022

The 2022 4DN Annual Meeting is open to 4DN Phase II funded members only (RFA-RM-20-XXX) and associate members.


Click here to register to the conference and submit an abstract

Each funded group is asked to give a progress report on their project as podium presentation. An abstract associated with your grant number has to be submitted before the deadline below. Any members of your group, not just the contact PI, can submit this abstract.

Additional abstracts can be submitted by every attendee for poster presentation.


Opening dates and deadlines

July 1, 2022 Registration and abstract submission open
July 1, 2022 Hotel booking opens (click here to book your hotel)
October 21, 2022 Abstract submission deadline
November 16, 2022 Hotel booking deadline

Lodging

Attendees are responsible for their lodging expenses. There is no delegated travel company who are tasked to contact attendees and book the hotel rooms.
